🧠 Brain Anatomy Overview

The human brain is the control center of the body, responsible for thoughts, memory, emotions, and vital functions.

🧩 Cerebrum

Controls thinking, learning, memory, speech, emotions, and voluntary movements.

⚖️ Cerebellum

Responsible for balance, posture, coordination, and smooth muscle movements.

🫀 Brainstem

Controls vital functions such as breathing, heartbeat, blood pressure, and sleep.

🧬 Common Brain Tumor Types

Glioma

Arises from glial cells. Can be aggressive and may affect thinking or movement.

Meningioma

Develops from brain membranes. Usually benign and slow growing.

Pituitary Tumor

Affects hormone regulation and vision due to gland compression.

No Tumor

Normal brain scan without abnormal tissue growth.

⚠️ Symptoms & Diagnosis

Common Symptoms
  • Persistent headaches
  • Seizures
  • Vision or speech problems
  • Memory loss or confusion
  • Hormonal imbalance
Diagnosis Methods
  • MRI & CT scans
  • Biopsy
  • Neurological examination
  • Hormone blood tests

🛡️ Brain Health & Prevention

While not all brain tumors are preventable, maintaining a healthy lifestyle can support overall brain health.

✔ Regular medical checkups ✔ Avoid radiation exposure ✔ Healthy diet & sleep ✔ Stress management
